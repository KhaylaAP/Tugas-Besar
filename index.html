You've might seen some handicraft products before that are usually made from yarn. These items are made by using either a Crochet method or a Knit method. While most people are more commonly aware of knitting, crochet is slightly similar and yet very different to it. When you knit you would use at minimum 2 knitting needles, while in crochet you would use only 1 crochet hook. Knitting is also mainly used to make clothing items like socks, sweaters, mittens, hats, etc. You can also use crochet to make those items as well as other things like plushies.

Another interesting fact about crochet is that it cannot be replicated by machines since it is three-dimensional and that the stitches have many layers so it requires a lot of dexterity during the process. On the other hand, knitting machines exist and is widely used in a semi or fully automatic fashion. While both are unique in their own ways, they each also have their own pros and cons that might make you want to try one more than the other.
