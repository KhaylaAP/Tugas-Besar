Crochet Patterns


Mini Octopus


Small Pouch

Materials

  • Crochet Hook
  • Yarn
  • Polyfill
  • Safety Eyes
  • Stitch Marker / Scrap Yarn
  • Needle
  • Scissors

Pattern

by @crochetedbyel on Instagram

Body

R1: 7 SC in MR (7)
R2: INC x7 (14)
R3: (SC, INC) x7 (21)
R4: (SC 2, INC) (28)
R5: (SC 3, INC) (35)
R6: SC 35 (35)
R7: SC 35 (35)
R8: SC 35 (35)
R9: SC 35 (35)
R10: SC 35 (35)
R11: SC 35 (35)
R12: (SC 3, DEC) x7 (28)
R13: (SC 2, DEC) x7 (21)
R14 in FLO: (in the same stitch HDC, DC 2, HDC, in next stitch SL ST) x11
Place 2 eyes on row 10, 8 ST apart

Bottom Piece

R1: 7 SC in MR (7)
R2: INC x7 (14)
R3: (SC, INC) x7 (21)

Ears x2

R1: 5 SC in MR (5)

Assembly

Stuff the inside with polyfill
Sew on the bottom piece on the open hole at the bottom
Sew on the ears between row 5-7
Cut and weave in all the remaining yarn
Your octopus is now finished!




Materials

  • Crochet Hook
  • Yarn
  • Needle
  • Scissors

Pattern

R1: MR, CH 2, DC 12, SL ST (12)
R2: CH 2, DC INC x12 (24)
R3: CH 2, (DC, DC INC) x12 (36)
R4: CH 2, BLO DC 36, SL ST (36)
R5: CH 2, DC 36, change to 2nd color, SL ST (36)
R6: CH 2, DC 36, SL ST (36)
R7: CH 2, DC 36, change to 3rd color, SL ST (36)
R8: CH 2, DC 36, SL ST (36)
R9: CH 2, DC 36, change to 4th color, SL ST (36)
R10: CH 2, DC 36, SL ST (36)
R11: CH 2, DC 36, change to 5th color, SL ST (36)
R12: CH 3, TR 36, SL ST (36)
R13: CH 2, DC 36, SL ST (36)

Tie

R1: CH 65 (65)

Assembly

Thread Tie through row 12 to make the drawstring
Your pouch is now finished!

  • Abbreviations
  • MR - magic ring
  • CH - chain
  • SC - single crochet
  • INC - increase
  • DEC - decrease
  • HDC - half double crochet
  • DC - double crochet
  • TR - treble crochet
  • SL ST - slip stitch
  • FLO - front loop only
  • BLO - back loop only
