Knitting Patterns


Socks


Fingerless Mittens

Materials

  • 5 Double Pointed Knitting Needles
  • Yarn
  • Stich Markers
  • Needle
  • Scissors

Pattern

by @CrazySockLady on Youtube

For size S
Body

CO 56 sts
Evenly distribute the stitches across four needles, join in the round
Knit 1x1 rib for ~4.5cm
Knit in the round even for ~4cm
Knit across needle 1 and needle 2

Heel Flap

(SL ST purlwise with yarn in the back 1, knit 1) across needle 3 and needle 4
turn the work, SL ST purwise with yarn in the front 1, Purl across
Repeat the last 2 steps x14 (Total 28 rows)

Heel Turn

SL ST purlwise with yarn in the back 1, K14, SSK, K1
turn the work, SL ST purwise with yarn in the front 1, P3, P2tog, P1
turn the work, SL ST purlwise with yarn in the back 1, Knit to 1 stitch before the gap, SSK, K 1
turn the work, SL ST purwise with yarn in the front 1, Purl to 1 stitch before the gap, P2tog, P 1
Repeat those 2 rows until you have 2 unworked stitches on each side
turn the work, Purl to 1 stitch before the gap, P2tog, P1

Gusset

K8 on one needle, K8 on another needle
Pick up 14 sts on the side of the heel flap, pick up 1 st on the ladder
Knit across needle 1 and needle 2
Pick up 1 st on the ladder, pick up 14 sts on the side of the heel flap, knit across needle 3
Knit across needle 4 until 3 sts before the end of the needle, K2tog, K1, knit across needle 1 & 2, K1, SSK, knit across
Knit 1 round even
Repeat the last 2 steps until each needle has 14 sts
Knit across needle 4
Knit in the round for ~12cm

Toe

K1, SSK, knit across needle 1, knit across needle 2 until the last 3 stitches, K2tog, K1, K1, SSK, knit across needle 3, knit across needle 4 until the last 3 stitches, K2tog, K1
Knit 1 round even
Repeat until there are 7 sts per needle

K1, SSK, knit across needle 1, knit across needle 2 until the last 3 stitches, K2tog, K1, K1, SSK, knit across needle 3, knit across needle 4 until the last 3 stitches, K2tog, K1
Repeat until there are 4 sts per needle

Kitchener

Combine needle 1 & 2, combine needle 3 & 4
Cut a long tail yarn, thread through tapestry needle
With tapestry needle K1 the front needle, take off the stitch, P1 the front needle
P1 the back needle, take off stitch, K1 the back needle
Repeat until there are no more stitches on either needles
Weave in ends

Your sock is now finished!




Materials

  • 4 Double Pointed Knitting Needles
  • Yarn

Pattern

by @sheepandstitch on Youtube

For size M

CO 48 sts
Evenly distribute the stitches across three needles, join in the round
Knit 2x2 rib for ~5cm
K 22 sts, PM, K 4, PM, Knit to the end
Knit 1 round even
Knit 1 round even
(Increase Round)
Knit to marker, SM, M1-L, knit to the next marker, M1-R, SM, knit to the end

  1. Knit 1 round even
  2. Knit 1 round even
  3. Knit 1 increase round
  1. Knit 1 round even
  2. Knit 1 round even
  3. Knit 1 increase round
Total 54 sts, 10 sts between markers
  1. Knit 1 round even
  2. Knit 1 round even
  3. Knit 1 round even
  4. Knit 1 increase round
  1. Knit 1 round even
  2. Knit 1 round even
  3. Knit 1 round even
  4. Knit 1 increase round
  1. Knit 1 round even
  2. Knit 1 round even
  3. Knit 1 round even
  4. Knit 1 increase round
Total 60 sts, 16 sts between markers


Thumb Set up

Knit to marker, remove marker, place 16 sts onto scrap yarn, remove marker
CO 1 st, rejoin for working in the round, knit to the end of the round
Total 45 sts on needle, 16 sts on scrap yarn

  1. Knit 1 round even
  2. Knit 1 round even
  3. Knit 1 round even
  4. Knit 1 round even
  5. Knit 1 round even
  6. Knit 1 round even
  7. Knit 1 round even
  8. Knit 1 round even
K2tog, Knit to end (44)

  1. Knit 2x2 rib in the round
  2. Knit 2x2 rib in the round
  3. Knit 2x2 rib in the round
  4. Knit 2x2 rib in the round
  5. Knit 2x2 rib in the round
  6. Knit 2x2 rib in the round
  7. Knit 2x2 rib in the round
Cast Off loosely

Thumb

Place 16 thumb sts onto needle
Evenly distribute the stitches across 3 needles
Take off scrap yarn
Rejoin working yarn
Knit 1 round even
Pick up and knit 2 sts at the end of the round where there is a gap (18)
Knit 1 round even

  • Abbreviations
  • sts - stitches
  • CO - Cast On
  • K - Knit
  • P - Purl
  • SL ST - Slip Stitch
  • PM - Place (stitch) Marker
  • SM - Slip (stitch) Marker
  • M1-L - Make 1 Left
  • M1-R - Make 1 Right
  • K2tog - Knit 2 Together
  • P2tog - Purl 2 Together
  • SSK - Slip Slip Knit
