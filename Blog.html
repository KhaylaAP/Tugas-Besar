How to Crochet

If you are intersted in learning how to crochet, here you'll be able to get to know about the basics of crochet and maybe even start your own project! It may seem hard at first but once you get the hang of it, you'll be able to make a lot of useful or cute items by hand! Don't be afraid to make mistakes as well, with enough practice you'll be able to understand the different stitches and how to read patterns.

Before we start there are a few things you'll need to start crocheting. These items are: a crochet hook, some yarn, a needle, and a pair of scissors. Out of all of these items the two most important are the crochet hook and the yarn. There are various types of hooks and yarn and to make sure the size of your hook matches your yarn, make sure to read the yarn label! Once you know what size hook you'll need, it is recommended that you find a crochet hook with a comfortable handle and the hook is made out of metal because, while there are wooden, plastic, and bamboo hooks, with metal the yarn will be able to glide easier on the hook.

How to Knit

If you want to learn how to knit, there are two main stitches that you will need to learn and that will allow you to do more beautiful and complex stitches. By learning how to knit, you can make simple things like a scarf or a blanket, to something like a hat or socks. While knitting looks easy, it is be inevitable that you will make mistakes, but don't get disheartend and keep learning because once you get the hang of it, you will be able to knit without having to think much.

To start knitting you will need a few things to start off. You will need knitting needles, some yarn, a tapestry needle, and a measuring tape if you want to make clothing items. First things first though, there are a few types of knitting needles that you can get depending on what you want to make but the most beginner friendly type is the double pointed knitting needle like the one in the image. These needles are very versitle because you can make products that are flat with only two needles and products that are in the round with four or five of these needles.
